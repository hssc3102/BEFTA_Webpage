Oil Palm

Despite the negative public image palm oil has, it is one of the most versatile agricultural products produced worldwide and has potential to be sustainably managed. Compared to other oil crops which are thought to be more eco-friendly options (e.g. Sunflower or rapseeed), it has a much higher yield per hectare and is replanted every 20 years, meaning it is less impactful on the ambient environment (e.g. soil conditions).
