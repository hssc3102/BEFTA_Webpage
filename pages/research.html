RERTA Project

The Riparian Ecosystem Restoration in Tropical Agriculture (RERTA) Project, established in 2018...

  • Sampling locations:
    • Within riparian margin
    • 50m from river
    • 500m into landscape
  • Treatments:
    • A: Enrichment + mature buffer
    • B: No restoration + mature buffer
    • C: Enrichment + remove mature oil palm
    • D: No restoration + replant
