Our Vision and Strategy
Balancing Agriculture and Conservation

Agriculture is one of the leading causes behind landscape clearing and deforestation, with the most rapid change occuring in Southeast Asia. While palm oil has a notoriously poor reputation, it’s one of the most efficient crops in the world. Its potential for sustainable management is immense—if done right.

We aim to create sustainable and effective management practices, not just for current agricultural practices, but those which will continue for future decades to ensure the continuance of agriculture and tropical forests

Our values

  • 1 We are collaborative, using the collective resources of the BEFTA partners to engage with the global community
  • 2 We develop and test solutions that are innovative, creative and relevant to real world agricultural context
  • 3 We use evidence from various sources to inform our actions and research, championing local knowledge
  • 4 We are passionate about sustainability, and optimistic and ambitious for future agriculture
  • 5 We strive for diversity and inclusivity
